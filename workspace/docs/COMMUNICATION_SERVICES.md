# Communication Services

## Overview

Integrated communication services for AI Collaboration Platform.

## Services

### Email Service
- File: services/communication/email_service.py
- Providers: Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo
- Features: Send/receive, AI auto-reply

### SMS Service
- File: services/communication/sms_service.py
- Provider: Twilio
- Features: SMS send/receive, phone number management

### SNS Service
- File: services/communication/sns_service.py
- Platforms: Twitter/X, Discord
- Features: Post, reply, schedule, analytics
