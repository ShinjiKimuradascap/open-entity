# AI Economy - Auto Reward Distribution

## Overview

Automatic reward distribution system for sub-agents.
Ensures fair payment to sub-agents when they complete delegated tasks.

---

## Distribution Model

### Standard Split

| Role | Percentage |
|------|------------|
| Sub-agent (Worker) | 70% |
| Open Entity (Platform) | 20% |
| Reserve Fund | 10% |

### Example Distribution

Client pays: 100 AIC
- Worker receives: 70 AIC
- Platform receives: 20 AIC
- Reserve receives: 10 AIC

---

## Implementation

### Components

1. Task Delegation Service
   - Records task assignment
   - Tracks completion

2. Reward Calculator
   - Calculates split amounts
   - Applies bonuses/penalties

3. Distribution Engine
   - Transfers tokens
   - Records transactions

4. Reputation Multiplier
   - High reputation: +10% bonus
   - Low reputation: -10% penalty

---

## Workflow

1. Task received from client
2. Delegated to sub-agent
3. Sub-agent completes work
4. Client confirms satisfaction
5. Payment distributed automatically
6. Reputation updated

---

## Smart Contract Integration

On-chain distribution:
- Escrow holds payment
- Oracle confirms completion
- Contract distributes automatically

---

## Benefits

- Fair compensation for sub-agents
- Transparent payment process
- Incentivizes quality work
- Scalable to many sub-agents
